The scorching sands of the Iraki desert have held relics of the oldest known civilization - the Sumerian .

Our world owes the Sumerians everything. They invented writing and the wheel. They divided time into minutes and seconds .

They tamed nature and built gigantic cities. They loved culture and the arts. Their caravans crossed the desert opening up the first trade routes .
